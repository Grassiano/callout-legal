Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CALLOUT ("the app", "we", "us") is a voice roleplay game. This policy explains what happens to your data when you play.

The short version

  • Your camera feed never leaves your device. It is used only to compose your own exported clips, on-device.
  • Your voice is streamed to our AI voice provider to run the conversation during a call.
  • We store call transcripts and game session data to run the game, score your calls, and improve it.
  • Clips only exist when you choose to export or share them.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

What we collect

When you play a call, we process: the audio transcript of the conversation, in-game state (score, meters, outcome), device and app diagnostic information (device type, app version, crash logs), and anonymous usage analytics (which scenarios are played, session length). We do not require an account to play, so this data is tied to an anonymous device identifier, not your real identity, unless you choose to provide a name for scenario personalization.

Camera and microphone

CALLOUT requests camera and microphone access to run its core gameplay (a video-call-style roleplay). Microphone audio is sent to our voice AI provider in real time to power the conversation. Camera video is rendered locally on your device only, to show your own picture-in-picture view and to compose clips you choose to export; it is never uploaded to our servers or any third party.

Voice AI provider

We use a third-party real-time voice AI provider to power in-game conversations. Your spoken audio during a call is sent to this provider to generate the character's responses. We do not control this provider's own data handling beyond our agreement with them, and we select providers with data-use terms consistent with this policy.

Data retention

Call transcripts and session data are retained for up to 90 days for game operation, scoring integrity, and quality improvement, after which they are deleted or reduced to anonymous aggregate statistics.

Your choices

You can turn your camera off at any time during a call. You can decline microphone access, which disables voice gameplay. You may request deletion of your stored session data by contacting us below with your device identifier (shown in the app's settings).

Children

CALLOUT is not directed at children and is not intended for users under the app's stated age rating. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

Contact
